## Pre-Trade Checklist (Before Every Trade)

### Setup Validation
□ Setup matches my trading plan criteria
□ Trade is in direction of higher timeframe trend
□ Clear support/resistance level involved
□ Entry trigger has occurred

### Risk Check
□ Position size calculated correctly
□ Stop loss placed before entry
□ Risk is ≤ ___% of account
□ Not already at maximum daily risk

### Mental Check
□ I am calm and focused
□ This is NOT a revenge trade
□ This is NOT FOMO
□ I accept I may lose this trade
