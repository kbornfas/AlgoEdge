# PRICE ACTION TRADING CHEAT SHEET
## Quick Reference for Clean Chart Trading

---

## MARKET STRUCTURE

### Uptrend
- Higher Highs (HH) + Higher Lows (HL)
- ONLY look for LONGS
- Buy at HL, target HH

### Downtrend
- Lower Highs (LH) + Lower Lows (LL)
- ONLY look for SHORTS
- Sell at LH, target LL

### Range
- Equal highs and lows
- Sell at resistance, buy at support
- Or trade the breakout

---

## STRUCTURE SIGNALS

### Break of Structure (BOS)
- Trend CONTINUATION signal
- Price breaks previous high (bullish) or low (bearish)
- Look for entry on retest

### Change of Character (CHoCH)
- Potential trend REVERSAL signal
- In uptrend: First Lower Low
- In downtrend: First Higher High
- Wait for confirmation before trading

---

## CANDLESTICK SIGNALS

### Pin Bar (Hammer/Shooting Star)
```
Bullish:        Bearish:
   |               |
   □            ===|===
===|===            □
   |               |
   |               |
```
- Long wick = rejection
- Enter: Beyond body
- Stop: Beyond wick

### Engulfing
```
Bullish:      Bearish:
  □           ■■■
 ■■■           □
■■■■■        ■■■■■
■■■■■          □
```
- Second body engulfs first
- Strong reversal signal
- Enter: On close

### Inside Bar
```
  |
=====   <-- Mother bar
  |
  ==    <-- Inside bar
  |
```
- Consolidation/coiling
- Trade the breakout
- Stop: Opposite side of mother bar

## KEY LEVELS

### Support
- Previous swing lows
- Demand zones
- Round numbers
- Look for LONGS here

### Resistance
- Previous swing highs
- Supply zones
- Round numbers
- Look for SHORTS here

### The Flip
- Broken support → New resistance
- Broken resistance → New support
- Very powerful levels

---

## SUPPLY & DEMAND

### Demand Zone (Buy Zone)
- Area where price exploded UP from
- Mark the base before the move
- Buy when price returns

### Supply Zone (Sell Zone)
- Area where price exploded DOWN from
- Mark the base before the move
- Sell when price returns

## ENTRY CHECKLIST

Before ANY trade:
□ What is the higher TF trend?
□ Is price at a key level?
□ Is there a candlestick signal?
□ Is my R:R at least 1:2?
□ Where is my stop (beyond structure)?
□ Where is my target (next key level)?

---

## STOP LOSS RULES

1. Beyond the structure that invalidates trade
2. Beyond the signal candle wick
3. Add buffer (5-15 pips)
4. Should be 1-2x ATR minimum

---

## MULTI-TIMEFRAME

### Higher TF (D1/H4)
→ Determines DIRECTION

### Trading TF (H1/M15)
→ Finds SETUP

### Entry TF (M5/M1)
→ Times ENTRY

**Trade in direction of higher TF only!**

---

## COMMON MISTAKES

❌ Trading against higher TF trend
❌ No clear candlestick signal
❌ Stop loss at obvious level
❌ Not waiting for confirmation
❌ Trading at random levels
❌ Forcing trades

## THE PRICE ACTION MANTRA

1. IDENTIFY the trend
2. WAIT for pullback to key level
3. LOOK for candlestick confirmation
4. ENTER with stop beyond structure
5. MANAGE with price action
6. EXIT at target or trail
