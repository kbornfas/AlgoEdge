# DAILY TRADING PSYCHOLOGY CHECKLIST
## Print and Use Before Every Trading Session

---

## PRE-MARKET MENTAL PREP

### Physical State Check
□ I got enough sleep (7+ hours)
□ I have eaten/will eat a healthy meal
□ I am hydrated
□ I have exercised or stretched
□ I am not ill or in pain

### Emotional State Check (Rate 1-10)
□ Calmness level: ___/10
□ Focus level: ___/10  
□ Confidence level: ___/10
□ Stress level: ___/10 (lower is better)

**If any score is poor, consider reduced trading or no trading.**

### Life Circumstances Check
□ No major personal issues distracting me
□ No financial pressure to perform
□ No time pressure today
□ I can afford to lose today's risk

---

## PRE-TRADE CHECKLIST

### Before Every Trade, Ask:

**Setup Quality**
□ Is this an A-grade setup from my playbook?
□ Would I take this trade if I were starting fresh?
□ Is there clear, logical reasoning for this trade?

**Emotional Check**
□ Am I calm and focused right now?
□ Am I NOT revenge trading?
□ Am I NOT trading out of boredom?
□ Am I NOT trading FOMO?
□ Am I NOT trading to impress anyone?

**Risk Check**
□ I have calculated my position size correctly
□ My stop loss is in place
□ I accept that I may lose this trade
□ Losing this amount will NOT hurt me

**Final Question**
□ If this trade loses, will I still be proud of taking it?

**If all boxes are checked: Take the trade**
**If any box is unchecked: DO NOT TRADE**

---

## POST-TRADE REVIEW

### After Every Trade:

**Execution Quality**
□ I followed my entry rules
□ I followed my exit rules
□ I did not move my stop loss
□ I did not revenge trade

**Emotional Review**
- How did I feel during the trade? _______________
- Did any emotions affect my decisions? ___________
- What would I do differently? ___________________

---

## END OF DAY REVIEW

### Daily Scorecard

**Process Score** (more important than P/L)
- Rules followed: ___/10
- Emotional control: ___/10
- Setup selection: ___/10
- **Process Average: ___/10**

**Results** (secondary)
- Total trades: ___
- Winners: ___
- Losers: ___
- P/L: $___

**Key Question**
If I trade like this every day, will I be profitable long-term?
□ Yes → Keep doing what I'm doing
□ No → What needs to change?

---

## QUICK EMOTIONAL RESET

If you feel emotional during trading:

1. **STOP** - Step away from computer
2. **BREATHE** - 4 counts in, 4 hold, 4 out (x4)
3. **WALK** - Move your body for 5 minutes
4. **ASSESS** - Am I in a state to trade well?
5. **DECIDE** - Return to trade or stop for the day

---

## WARNING SIGNS TO WATCH

Stop trading if you notice:
⚠️ Checking charts every few seconds
⚠️ Heart racing during trades
⚠️ Feeling angry at the market
⚠️ Wanting to "get back" at losses
⚠️ Taking trades not in your plan
⚠️ Moving stop losses to avoid loss
⚠️ Feeling desperate to make money
⚠️ Trading larger than usual

Read before trading:
