# SUPPORT & RESISTANCE CHEAT SHEET
## Quick Reference Guide for Key Level Trading

---

## LEVEL TYPES

### 1. Horizontal Levels
- Previous swing highs/lows
- Multiple touches = stronger
- Draw zones, not lines (10-20 pips)

### 2. Round Numbers
**Major**: 1.0000, 1.1000 (00 levels)
**Minor**: 1.0500, 1.1500 (50 levels)
**Gold**: 1900, 2000, 2100

### 3. Dynamic Levels (Moving Averages)
- 20 EMA: Short-term
- 50 EMA: Medium-term
- 200 EMA: Long-term

### 4. Trend Lines
- Connect 2+ swing points
- 3rd touch = tradeable
- Angle ~45° is ideal

## TRADING AT SUPPORT

### Entry Criteria
□ Price at significant support
□ Bullish candle pattern (hammer, engulfing)
□ RSI oversold (<30)
□ With trend direction

### Trade Setup
- Entry: Close above bullish candle
- Stop: Below support zone (10-15 pips buffer)
- Target: Next resistance

---

## TRADING AT RESISTANCE

### Entry Criteria
□ Price at significant resistance
□ Bearish candle pattern (shooting star, engulfing)
□ RSI overbought (>70)
□ With trend direction

### Trade Setup
- Entry: Close below bearish candle
- Stop: Above resistance zone (10-15 pips buffer)
- Target: Next support

---

## SUPPORT/RESISTANCE FLIP

When support breaks → becomes resistance
When resistance breaks → becomes support

**Trading the Flip**:
1. Wait for level to break
2. Wait for price to retest
3. Enter on rejection of retest
4. Stop beyond the level

---

## LEVEL DRAWING RULES

1. ✅ Use zones, not exact lines
2. ✅ Less is more (only major levels)
3. ✅ Start on higher timeframe
4. ✅ Prioritize recent levels
5. ✅ Look for multiple touches
6. ❌ Don't force levels
7. ❌ Don't mark every swing

---

## COMMON MISTAKES

❌ Drawing too many levels
❌ Using exact prices (use zones)
❌ Ignoring higher timeframe levels
❌ Trading against the trend at levels
❌ Not waiting for confirmation

---

## QUICK CHECKLIST

Before trading a level:
□ Level marked on higher TF?
□ Multiple touches?
□ Confirmation candle present?
□ With or against trend?
□ Risk/reward acceptable?
