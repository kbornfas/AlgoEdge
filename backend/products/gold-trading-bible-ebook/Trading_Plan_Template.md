## SECTION 9: EMERGENCY PROTOCOLS

### If I Hit Maximum Daily Loss:
1. Close all positions immediately
2. Close trading platform
3. Do not trade for rest of day
4. Review what went wrong
5. Get proper rest

### If I Hit Maximum Drawdown:
1. Stop trading completely
2. Take 2-3 days off minimum
3. Review all trades that contributed
4. Identify pattern or problem
5. Create action plan before returning
6. Return with reduced size (50%)

### If I Break Major Rules:
1. Acknowledge the violation
2. Document what happened
3. Identify the trigger
4. Create prevention plan
5. Consider demo trading for 1 week

### If I Feel Emotional:
1. Step away from screens
2. Take 10 deep breaths
3. Walk around for 5 minutes
4. Only return when calm
5. Reduce position size if still uneasy
